Puddings

For Group B

Add calories to milk by adding 4 tablespoons of dried milk powder to each pint of fresh full fat milk (This is called fortified milk).

Add double cream, ice cream, fortified milk or evaporated milk to puddings, fruit or instant desserts.

Add syrup, honey or sugar to ice cream or other puddings.

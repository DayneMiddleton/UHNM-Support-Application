Fruit and Vegetables

For Group A

Aim to eat five pieces of fruit and veg a day.

1 portion = 80g or is equivalent to an apple, or banana or 2 small plums.

Vegetables should make up at least 1/3 of your meal. They can be frozen, tinned or fresh.

Fruit can be stewed, fresh, frozen or tinned.
